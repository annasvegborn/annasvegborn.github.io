Click on the game canvas to activate arrow keys! How to play: Click on the car you wish to drive. Change lane with the left and right arrow keys or click on the the lane to jump to. Avoid the car tire and the tree. Hamburgers give you bonus points. Pause by clicking at the right top corner or key button S. Start by clicking at the left top corner or key button A. Remember that the more points you get the faster the car drives. Reload the web browser to start over the game.
